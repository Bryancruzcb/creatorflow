Why a checklist alongside the map. The Release Flow graph is the coordination view — parallel work and downstream impact. This checklist is the go/no-go view for one candidate: the seven things a reviewer signs off before publish, with the two that no first-party tool does well — a real asset permission diff against what shipped last, and an exact rollback target backed by immutable animation snapshots — pulled to the front. Similarity remains a review lead, never an authorship or infringement verdict. Nothing here uploads to Roblox; the final steps hand off to Studio and record what came back.
